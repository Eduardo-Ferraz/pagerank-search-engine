of standing mast-heads ashore or afloat is a very ancient and interesting one let us in some measure expatiate here I take it that the earliest standers of mast-heads were the old Egyptians because in all my researches I find none prior to them For though their progenitors the builders of Babel must doubtless by their tower have intended to rear the loftiest mast-head in all Asia or Africa either yet ere the final truck was put to it as that great stone mast of theirs may be said to have gone by the board in the dread gale of God s wrath therefore we cannot give these Babel builders priority over the Egyptians And that the Egyptians were a nation of mast-head standers is an assertion based upon the general belief among archseo- 191 192 MOBY-DICK legists that the first pyramids were
